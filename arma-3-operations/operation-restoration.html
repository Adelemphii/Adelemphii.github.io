Briefing

Our group HQ lies upon an island off the east coast of Africa, one of many small subsidiaries from the mainland nation of Baltan. Our goal is to liberate the small subsidiaries to build up supplies and public support, thus moving forward to bring down the government. Firstmost, we require more combative supplies. The National Police force has received support from unknown suppliers, with equipment of old hand-me-down NATO variety. Unsurprisingly they’ve been using it to suppress any small militia and rebellion group.

We require those supplies. Our group has no short range, and has limited long range radios for the leaders of each squad. Thus, I have marked an objective on your maps. Objective 1, a guarded police compound containing supplies. We have little information on what sort of weaponry they have, however there has been no sighted AT or explosives being used by the National Police… It seems they’re at least reasonable.

There are multiple checkpoints across the island, manned by members of the National Police. Be wary of bringing those M2 trucks near them, that’ll likely lead to conflict. The National Police aren’t knowledgeable about our group yet, thus leaving the opportunity of scouting before engaging.

Now, I will mention, there is one military outpost which I have marked upon your maps, towards the northern end. They’re stationed around a radio tower, which is important as it allows for contact with external forces apart from the island. I’ve marked it on your maps. They’re believed to be heavily armed, and if attacked they’re likely to send a signal out. Granted, the technology isn’t great here, so it’ll take a few minutes for a message to be sent. You’d best destroy the tower before then.

Oh, and before you go… There’s been reports of a military shipment being received down south, looks like a possible supply run to the northern radio station. A convoy alongside the west side of the island is a probable occurrence. It won't happen for some time, and I’ll keep a scout to bring word for when it's moving, if you wish to attack it. Let me reiterate, stealth is optional. You can probably disguise yourself and get away with some things. Our primary goal is to increase our status among the public and to gain supplies, so when you clear towns give me a shout and tag the supplies with spray paint. I’ll send a team to retrieve it.

However you plan to go about it, is entirely up to you. Just bring back some good results eh?
